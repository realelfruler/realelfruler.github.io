about me

hi! my name is miles asher (he/him), and i'm an artist, writer, and amateur coder living in the united states.

right now, my favorite media is ace attorney, and my favorite character is klavier gavin! (though, i do very much enjoy apollo justice as well...). i also like the game dredge. i enjoy listening to 80s music, disco, and the band the flaming lips.

currently, i'm a college student, working to get a degree in anthropology and sociology, with a minor (or possibly a major) in religious studies.

right now, i'm an intern at a local history museum, doing research and assisting in creating exhibits for the 250th anniversary of the declaration of independence. i know a lot of obscure history stuff, and am always excited to do research, so if you want to talk to me about american history or religion, send me an ask on tumblr!

my art

Dune Family Portrait

this painting was made to be featured on my character blog, 'Ask Fechin', to be used in one of his posts. more broadly, 'Ask Fechin' is the social media of a seelie fae named fechin dune-affean, who is the ruler of a kingdom called the dappled forest, and he is one of the main characters of a story i'm writing with friends called 'For All Eternity,' or 'FAE' for short. in the painting, he's featured as a baby with his mother, the deposed queen chrysanthemum, and his father, prince consort rian, shortly after her coronation.
